Face Shield Info Page

In response to the Covid-19 Pandemic, makers the world over have responded in a tremendous way by designing and making various items that are sorely needed by the medical community but are in limited supply. One of these items is Face Shields.

I wanted to make the process of figuring out what to do, and doing it, as simple as possible, so I put together this little web site.
