Face Shield Info Page

In response to the Covid-19 Pandemic, makers the world over have responded in a tremendous way by designing and making various items that are sorely needed by the medical community but are in limited supply. One of these items is Face Shields.

I wanted to make the process of figuring out what to do, and doing it, as simple as possible, so I put together this little web site.

  • Distributing Them

    Of course these face shields are of little use if you can't get them to the people who need to use them! You don't want a box of them just sitting in your basement collecting dust!

    Below is a list of options you can explore...

    • Contact your local municipality and ask them if they have a need for these kind of shields.
    • Contact local hospitals.
    • Do you personally know people who would benefit from these shields? Doctors, nurses, people who have to work around others (one example that comes to mind is someone working a cash register at a store). Contact these people.
    • If any of the above options seems a bit much for you, Matterhackers has set up a special web page to help get to people who need the face shields to get them from the people (perhaps you?) who have made the face shields!
