Simple Steps to Make & Distribute Medical Face Shields Page

In response to the Covid-19 Pandemic, makers the world over have responded in a tremendous way by designing and making various items that are sorely needed by the medical community but are in a very limited supply. One of these items is Face Shields.

I wanted to make the process of figuring out what to do, and doing it, as simple as possible, so that I myself (as well as you too) could quickly see and understand what steps I need to take to make these face shields and get them to organizations that need them. So I put together this little web site!

Erik Cederburg has done an amazing work by creating lots of different options and versions for face shield frames, templates, files to be used with laser cutters to cut holes in the clear plastic sheets, etc. and placed them on-line for download.

It is really great to have all of these options, but...
it is a bit overwhelming. I wanted to print the 8 peg face shield frame Joel2 made in his YouTube video. It was really hard for me to find it! I did find it at last and download it by the way.   • Distributing Them

    Of course these face shields are of little use if you can't get them to the people who need to use them! You don't want a box of them just sitting in your basement collecting dust!

    Below is a list of options you can explore...

    • Contact your local municipality and ask them if they have a need for these kind of shields.
    • Contact local hospitals.
    • Do you personally know people who would benefit from these shields? Doctors, nurses, people who have to work around others (one example that comes to mind is someone working a cash register at a store). Contact these people.
    • If any of the above options seems a bit much for you, Matterhackers has set up a special web page (https://www.matterhackers.com/covid-19) to help get to people who need the face shields to get them from the people (perhaps you?) who have made the face shields!
